# Fool's Barrel

A Fabric mod that ports the "barrel on head" feature from the Minecraft April Fools' snapshot `22w13oneBlockAtATime` to modern versions. Wear a barrel and hide in plain sight!

## Features

- Barrels can be equipped to the head slot, playing the barrel-open sound on equip.
- Crouching while wearing a barrel counts as "hiding inside the barrel" and grants:
  - Hostile mobs (including the Warden) ignore you at the AI targeting level.
  - Other players and mobs will not lock their gaze onto you.
  - Creepers will not initiate their swell attack against you.
  - Matching client-side rendering: the player is visually hidden, with a barrel head overlay and eye-hole texture.
